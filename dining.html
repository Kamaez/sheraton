Worldly Cusine

Embark on a global culinary adventure at our exquisite restaurants. Savor the delicate art of Japanese Wagyu beef sizzling on a teppanyaki grill. Immerse yourself in the fragrant tapestry of Moroccan tagines, aromatic spices dancing on your palate. Delight in the rich history of Italian truffles shaved over hand-rolled pasta. Our world-class chefs, hailing from every corner of the globe, transform seasonal ingredients into a luxurious symphony of flavors, designed to tantalize and transport you on a journey around the world, all within the opulent walls of our magnificent hotel.
